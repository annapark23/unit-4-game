Crystals Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score. Amount of points will be hidden until the player clicks a crystal.

You will win by matching your total score to the random number assigned at the beginning of the game, you will lose by going over the random number.

Game will restart when the player wins or loses. New random number will be assigned.
